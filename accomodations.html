“Ongolog tour” tourist camp

Ongolog tour tourist camp, owned by WENS LLC, was established in 2010 in Khuvsgul Lake National Park. Located right on the shore of the lake, our camp is surrounded by pine trees and wild flowers.

Our tourist camp provides a traditional Mongolian Ger accommodation with hand-crafted wooden furniture and cozy wooden houses to our guests.
