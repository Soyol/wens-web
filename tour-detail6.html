Khuvsgul tour

Imagine having your picture taken while you ride on a reindeer during this unique and memorable trip!

Some environmentalists say that Husvgul Lake’s water is the purest in the world, rather than Baikal Lake. Want to decide for yourself? Take this short but action packed tour with us. You will be surrounded by natural beauty in the state park. Bring your camera, because there are plenty of gorgeous and rare vegetation and animals.

From hiking and horseback riding to the unique experience of riding on a reindeer, this is an experience you will never forget. Come to Mongolia and swim in some of the purest water in the world. The trip will be peaceful and you will return home refreshed and full of memories.

Duration

10 days/ 9 nights

Distance

1981 km

  • Day 1 Arrival. Ulaanbaatar

    Arrive in Ulaanbaatar, you will be welcomed by Ongolog tour guide and transfer to your centrally located Hotel. After lunch take a city tour to Gandan Monastery where you will see 25 meters tall gold plated statue of Magjid Janraisig. Gandan is the largest and most important monastery of Mongolia, with over 400 monks. Next, we will visit the National History Museum. Exhibition of museum covers history of Mongolia, ethnography and traditional life. Overnight at the hotel. (D)

    Dinner

  • Day 2 Amarbayasgalant

    Today we will drive to Amarbayasgalant Monastery which located in the Iven Valley near the Selenge River. Amarbayasgalant is one of the very few monasteries to have partly escaped destruction during the Stalinist purges of 1937. Overnight in a ger camp. (B+L+D)

    Breakfast Lunch Dinner

  • Day 3 Uran Togoo

    In the morning drive to Uran Togoo, an extinct volcano in Bulgan province. On the way we stop at Erdenet city for refueling and lunch. Erdenet is the third biggest city of Mongolia and this is big copper mining city. Overnight in a ger camp near Selenge River. (B+L+D)

    Breakfast Lunch Dinner

  • Day 4 Lake Khuvsgul

    Today we will drive, via Moron town, to Khuvsgul lake. Late afternoon we will arrive at our own Ongolog tour ger camp on the beautiful Khuvsgul lake shore. Overnight in a ger camp. (B+L+D)

    Breakfast Lunch Dinner

  • Day 5 Lake Khuvsgul

    Spend this day exploring the magnificent Khuvsgul Lake which is the second-most voluminous freshwater lake in Asia. There is an opportunity to go hiking, horse riding, ATV riding, and boat trips around the Lake. Overnight in a ger camp. (B+L+D)

    Breakfast Lunch Dinner

  • Day 6 Jargalant

    Today we will continue our journey & will arrive to the Jargal Jiguur hot spring in Jargalant soum. Jargal Jiguur has outdoor and indoor natural hot spa pool (separated man and women). This region is home of yaks and you can visit to yak herder family in here. Overnight in a ger camp. (B+L+D)

    Breakfast Lunch Dinner

  • Day 7 Khorgo, Terkhiin Tsagaan Nuur National park

    After breakfast, we drive to the magnificent volcanic area of Khorgiin Togoo, which is located on the eastern shore of Lake Terkhiin Tsagaan. Hike up to the summit of the volcanic crater for a breathtaking view of the surrounding landscape. Overnight in a ger camp on the nearby Lake. (B+L+D)

    Breakfast Lunch Dinner

  • Day 8 Kharkhorum and Erdenezuu

    In the morning, drive to Kharkhorum, which was the capital of the Mongol Empire between 1235 and 1260, and of the Northern Yuan in the 14–15th centuries and visit Erdene Zuu Buddhist Monastery, established on the ruins of Karakorum in 1586 by AbtaiSain Khan. Overnight in a ger camp. (B+L+D)

    Breakfast Lunch Dinner

  • Day 9 Ulaanbaatar

    Today we will drive back to Ulaanbaatar city and check into your hotel. On the way to Ulaanbaatar, you cross Elsentasarhai, an area consisting of mixes of all landscapes: mountains, sands and rivers. In the evening we will watch a live performance of Mongolian National folklore. Shopping for souvenir and cashmere is optional. Overnight at the hotel. (B+L+D)

    Breakfast Lunch Dinner

  • Day 10

    After breakfast, you will be transferred to the airport or train station for international departure.
