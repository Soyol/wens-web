Erdenet inn

Since 2005, Erdenet Inn has been offering comfortable stay for mainly tourists. Throughout the years, our business has expanded resulting in a much broader customer base, made up of foreign and national business people, government officials, and renowned figures.

We have created a comfortable, clean, and smoke free environment. Every room is equipped with cable, complimentary Wi-Fi, as well as interphone devices. Room rates include breakfast. Hotel guests can enjoy a 10% discount at Bella Spa, located on the 2nd floor.

There are a total of 22 rooms, consisting of Presidential room, premium room, deluxe room, and standard room.
