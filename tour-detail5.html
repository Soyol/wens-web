Khuvsgul tour (with flight)

Imagine having your picture taken while you ride on a reindeer during this unique and memorable trip!

Some environmentalists say that Husvgul Lakeâ€™s water is the purest in the world, rather than Baikal Lake. Want to decide for yourself? Take this short but action packed tour with us. You will be surrounded by natural beauty in the state park. Bring your camera, because there are plenty of gorgeous and rare vegetation and animals.

From hiking and horseback riding to the unique experience of riding on a reindeer, this is an experience you will never forget. Come to Mongolia and swim in some of the purest water in the world. The trip will be peaceful and you will return home refreshed and full of memories.

Duration

6 days/ 5 nights

Distance

Flight: 1060 km
Land: 440 km

Tour itinerary can be tailored per request

  • Day 1 Arrival. Ulaanbaatar

    Arrive in Ulaanbaatar, you will be welcomed by Ongolog tour guide and transfer to your centrally located Hotel. After lunch take a city tour to Gandan Monastery where you will see 25 meters tall gold plated statue of Magjid Janraisig. Gandan is the largest and most important monastery of Mongolia, with over 400 monks. Next, we will visit the National History Museum. Exhibition of museum covers history of Mongolia, ethnography and traditional life. Overnight at the hotel. (D)

    Dinner

  • Day 2 Flight to Murun, Khuvsgul Lake

    Flight to Murun, the center of Khuvsgul province. From the Murun Airport, we will start our journey to Khuvsgul Lake National park. Overnight in our own Ongolog tour ger camp on the lake shore. (B+L+D)

    Breakfast Lunch Dinner

  • Day 3 Lake Khuvsgul

    Spend this day exploring the magnificent Khuvsgul Lake which is the second-most voluminous freshwater lake in Asia. There is an opportunity to go hiking, horse riding, ATV riding, and boat trips around the Lake. Overnight in a ger camp. (B+L+D)

    Breakfast Lunch Dinner

  • Day 4 Flight to Ulaanbaatar. Terelj National Park

    Today we flight back to Ulaanbaatar. Then we drive to Terelj National Park, one of the most visited sites near Ulaanbaatar. Terelj is set in the picturesque valley of granite mountains and the beautiful Terelj River. On the way to Terelj we stop at Tsonchinboldog to visit the Giant Statue of Chinggis Khan. Then, we visit the Turtle Rock and the Temple of Meditation. Hike around. Overnight in a ger camp. (B+L+D)

    Breakfast Lunch Dinner

  • Day 5 Ulaanbaatar

    Today we will drive back to Ulaanbaatar city and check into your hotel. In the evening we will watch a live performance of Mongolian National folklore. Shopping for souvenir and cashmere is optional. Overnight at the hotel. (B+L+D)

    Breakfast Lunch Dinner

  • Day 6

    After breakfast, you will be transferred to the airport or train station for international departure.

    Breakfast Lunch Dinner
