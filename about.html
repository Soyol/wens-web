WENS LLC was established in 1999 as hospitality, tourism, and auto repair focused company with 8 employees. After 15 years of hard work and improvement, we have become one of the leading service based company in Erdenet with more than 40 employees. WENS LLC is proud to manage Erdenet city’s major restaurant, day spa, hotel, coffee shop, as well as American goods’ market.

In every aspect of our business, we strive to offer quality products through pleasant customer service. WENS LLC is proud to be responsible for aiding in the improvement of local citizens’ lives as well as the society. In 2013, WENS LLC was honored as the “Customer’s Reliable Organization”.
